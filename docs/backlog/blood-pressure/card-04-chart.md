# Card 04: Chart

## Objective

Visualize blood pressure readings over time with a line chart.

## Scope

- Show systolic as one line
- Show diastolic as one line
- Plot readings over time

## Acceptance Criteria

- The chart clearly shows blood pressure trends over time
- Systolic and diastolic are visually distinct
- The chart is easy to interpret at a glance

## Notes

- Keep the chart implementation minimal and practical
- Avoid extra chart features that do not help the main trend view
