# Product

BPHealth is a personal health tracker for one user only.

## Purpose

The primary purpose of the app is to track blood pressure over time in a way that is quick to enter, easy to review, and private by default.

## What to track

- Blood pressure readings over time
- Whether I slept or took a nap during the day
- Whether I drank alcohol that day
- Whether I felt good and productive, or not

## Product principles

- Keep the app simple and fast
- Make privacy a core expectation
- Show blood pressure trends clearly over time
- Avoid multi-user support
- Favor low-friction daily use over feature depth
