# Card 01: Basic Patterns

## Objective

Surface a few simple data comparisons that are easy to understand.

## Scope

- Average BP on days with alcohol vs without
- Average BP on days slept vs not
- Keep the output optional and lightweight

## Acceptance Criteria

- The insights are easy to read at a glance
- The calculations stay simple and transparent
- No advanced analytics or predictive output is introduced

## Notes

- Keep this as a small, optional layer on top of the existing data
