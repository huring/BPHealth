# Card 02: Day Grouping

## Objective

Group timeline items by day so related entries are easier to review.

## Scope

- Group items by calendar day
- Show blood pressure and daily factors under the same day heading

## Acceptance Criteria

- Timeline items are grouped by day
- The day grouping is easy to follow
- The view still feels lightweight and fast

## Notes

- Keep grouping minimal and avoid complex collapsible structures unless needed
